Mini Mindful
Children's Yoga

At mini mindful children’s yoga we aim to nurture and empower young bodies and minds whilst having fun.

About

Based in South London, our classes explore yoga through engaging themes, adventures, activities and poses that support children’s physical, emotional, social and intellectual development. Each child’s unique abilities are welcomed and encouraged in a non-competitive and happy environment.

We use breathing and relaxation exercises to help children become calm and mindful in the busy and sometimes stressful world they live in.

Our children build self-confidence, connect with each other using partner and group poses, develop important social skills – all while learning to love themselves.

Mini Mindful Children’s yoga is run by a fully-trained teacher with over eight years’ primary and special needs experience. We work closely with local nurseries and primary schools to provide high quality yoga sessions and after-school clubs.

Emma

Emma has over eight years’ experience as a qualified primary and early years teacher and has been practicing yoga herself for many years.

During her time working in challenging inner city schools, Emma began to explore yoga in her classroom by introducing breathing exercises. She immediately noticed the positive effect these had on the children’s ability to manage emotions, focus on their learning and feel calm and relaxed.

Mini Mindful allows her to combine her passion for working with young children with her love of yoga and its benefits for health and wellbeing. Emma qualified as a children’s yoga instructor with YogaBeez

Classes

Children's yoga - ages 5-10 years


Our weekly classes are the perfect way to explore all aspects of yoga through fun themes, stories and games. Children build confidence, self-awareness, physical strength, flexibility and balance and learn how to relax, with techniques to practise at home.

Classes are based at Gaia Studio, Peckham on Thursday from 5-6pm during term time. £7 per class if paid in advance for a 6 week half-term block. £9 for drop in (subject to availability)

Yoga in schools and nurseries

Mini Mindful began in the classroom and continues to provide yoga and mindfulness in a variety of school and nursery settings.

With over eight years' experience as a primary school teacher, Emma offers yoga clubs after school or during the school day as part of the PE or PSCHE curriculum.

Classes support the development of physical, emotional, social and intellectual wellbeing.

Birthday parties

Yoga parties are a great way to celebrate your special day! Session will be suited to the birthday boy or girl's interests and include lots of yoga games. Emma will visit your home or a venue of your choice.
